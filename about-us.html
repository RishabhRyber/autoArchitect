Team Auto Architect

All Terrain Vehicle Design and Fabrication

Team Auto Architect is All Terrain Vehicle design and fabrication team of Siddaganga Institute Of Technology which participates in an international level ATV competition, BAJA.

Your small support may help us innovate better and will provide your business a chance to gain bits of popularity.

The Team has participated in various ATV Design and Design Innovation Contest across the country and recognized by various organisation and conglomerates. The Team is highly committed for the development of All terrain Vehicles.

Imagination 1.0 (2016)
  • Vehicle specification :
    • Vehicle weight reduced to 339 kg from 380.2kg
  • Competition results :
    • Secured 39th AIR in virtuals round in.
    • Cleared all the technical tests and successfully drove the vehicle in Endurance round for the first time.
Imagination 2.0 (2018)
  • Vehicle specification :
    • Vehicle weight significantly reduced to 278 .4 kgs.
    • Switched to much lighter air shocks -Fox float 3 from spring shocks.
    • Used FRP instead of sheet metals for body panels.
  • Competition results :
    • Secured 3rd rank in Karnataka in virtuals round.
Imagination 3.0 (2019)
  • Vehicle specification :
    • Weight of vehicle reduced to 200.6 kgs.
    • First time moved to automatic transmission -CVT from manual transmission.
    • Inhouse assembly of various components.
  • Competition results :
    • Secured 1st in Karnataka & 50th all over India in Virtuals round among 350 teams.
    • Secured 10th rank in design presentation in M-BAJA.
    • Secured 42nd position All over India.
Imagination 4.0 (2020)
  • Vehicle specification :
    • Vehicle weight reduced to 174.8 kgs.
    • PP sheets instead of FRP was used.
    • Fox float 3 evol r was used instead of fox float 3.
  • Competition results :
    • Cleared the Virtuals round holding 4th position in Karnataka and 83rd position in All over India.
    • Completed 19 laps in 4 hours Endurance run without any failure securing 9th AIR in Endurance Race.
    • Overall rank was 30th All over India.
    • Among top 7 teams All Over India to be shortlisted for innovation event.
Champion Of Champions 2.0 (2020)

First time participated in Champion of Champions season 2.0 organised by ISIE INDIA held at KL University at Vijayawada in Andhra Pradesh.

  • 1st rank overall.
  • 1st in Endurance race.
  • 1st in Design presentation.
  • 1st in Manoeuvrability.
